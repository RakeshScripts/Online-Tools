How a SIP Calculator Helps You Plan Finances

A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) calculator is an essential tool for Indian investors looking to grow their wealth through Mutual Funds. Instead of timing the market, a SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly, which helps in averaging out the cost of purchase through Rupee Cost Averaging.

This calculator uses the compound interest formula to show how small monthly savings can turn into a massive corpus over 10, 20, or 30 years. For example, a monthly SIP of just ₹5,000 at a 12% return rate can grow significantly due to the power of compounding.

Key Features of SIP

  • Low Entry Barrier: You can start investing with as little as ₹500 per month.
  • Compound Interest: Each month’s return is reinvested, leading to exponential growth.
  • Financial Discipline: Helps in achieving long-term goals like retirement or children's education.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is SIP better than a Lump Sum?

SIP is generally safer for retail investors as it reduces the risk of market volatility by spreading investments over time. It is ideal for salaried individuals.

What is a realistic return rate for SIP in India?

While past performance doesn't guarantee future results, Equity Mutual Funds in India have historically provided returns between 12% to 15% over the long term (7+ years).
