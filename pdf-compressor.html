Frequently Asked Questions

How does PDF compression work?

Our tool uses two methods: Lossless Structural Optimization (removing duplicate fonts and redundant metadata) and Lossy Image Downsampling (reducing the DPI of embedded images). This allows for significant size reduction while maintaining visual clarity.

Is my data safe?

Yes. Unlike other converters, we process your file locally. Your document is never uploaded to a cloud server; it stays in your browser's memory, ensuring 100% privacy for legal or sensitive documents.

Why is my file still large after compression?

If a PDF is already highly optimized or contains mostly vector graphics (like blueprints or text-only documents), further compression may be limited. Compression is most effective on documents containing high-resolution photos or scanned pages.

Which compression mode should I choose?

Best Quality is ideal for printing. Recommended is perfect for email attachments (meeting the typical 25MB limit). Extreme is best for archival or mobile viewing where storage is a priority.

Technical Capabilities

Our engine supports Linearization (Web Optimization) which allows large PDFs to be viewed page-by-page as they download. We also strip unnecessary object streams and compress cross-reference tables.
