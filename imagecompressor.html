How to Use the Image Compressor

Our Image Compressor helps you reduce the size of your images without losing much quality. It’s fast, free, and works entirely in your browser — no uploads to external servers, keeping your files secure and private.

Compress Images

Optimize your JPG, PNG, or WebP files to make them smaller and easier to share online or store on your device.

  1. Select the "Image Compressor" tool from the main menu.
  2. Click the upload box or drag and drop your image files directly into the area. You can add multiple images at once.
  3. Choose your desired compression level:
    • Basic: Slight compression to reduce size while keeping the highest quality.
    • Recommended: Balanced mode for smaller size with minimal visible quality loss — ideal for websites or sharing.
    • Extreme: Strong compression for maximum space saving, best for quick previews or small thumbnails.
  4. Click the "Compress Images" button to start processing.
  5. After compression, preview the results and click the "Download" button to save your optimized images.
