Precision Image Scaling

High-quality resizing requires sophisticated algorithms. Our tool uses Bicubic Interpolation logic to ensure that your photos remain sharp even after significant scaling. By processing everything in your browser, your original high-resolution data never leaves your device.

Why use Independent Mode?

Sometimes you need to fit an image into a specific container (like a banner or profile slot) regardless of its original shape. Independent mode allows you to stretch or squash pixels to your exact requirements.

What is Local Processing?

Unlike other tools, we don't upload your images to a server. This means your privacy is 100% guaranteed, and the resizing happens at the speed of your own hardware.
