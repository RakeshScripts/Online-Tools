How to Generate a QR Code?

A QR Code (Quick Response Code) is a type of barcode that can be scanned by smartphones to quickly access information. Our free generator allows you to create codes for various purposes instantly.

Supported QR Types

  • URLs: Send users to your website, social media profile, or online menu.
  • Contact: Create codes for phone numbers or email addresses to make it easy for clients to reach you.
  • Plain Text: Encode simple messages or passwords.

Why use our generator?

Unlike many other services, our QR Generator is completely free, never expires, and works entirely in your browser. This means your data is private and the codes are generated with High Error Correction (Level H), making them easier to scan even if the code is slightly damaged or printed poorly.
