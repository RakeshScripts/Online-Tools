How to Extract Pages from PDF

Our PDF Splitter tool allows you to isolate specific pages from a large document into a brand new PDF file. This is perfect for when you only need to share a small portion of a report or save a specific receipt from a multi-page document.

Secure Local Processing

Safety is our top priority. Unlike traditional online tools, your files are never uploaded to a cloud server. Everything happens directly in your browser using the pdf-lib library. Your data remains on your machine, making this tool safe for sensitive financial and legal documents.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use ranges?

To extract multiple pages, separate them with commas. For example, typing 1, 3, 5-7 will give you pages 1, 3, 5, 6, and 7.

Is there a limit to how many pages I can split?

No. Our tool is completely free with no limits on the number of pages or file size, provided your computer has enough memory to process the file.
